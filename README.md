## üí° Tips for Interviews

1. **Understand the problem** before jumping to solutions
2. **Think aloud** - explain your thought process
3. **Start with High-Level Design** then move to details
4. **Consider trade-offs** - discuss pros and cons of your approach
5. **Ask clarifying questions** - don't make assumptions
6. **Use SOLID principles** - show you understand best practices
7. **Discuss scalability** - how would your design handle growth?
8. **Test your design** - think about edge cases
